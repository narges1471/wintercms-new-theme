Vega Lite is a clean, fully responsive, and feature-packed free WordPress theme. It is well suited for personal, creative, business, photography and any other professional blogs or websites.

The theme is simple but full of options that allow you to customize many aspects of the layout. The Customizer gives you complete control over the home page layout, blog feed layout, posts, pages and other settings such as animation and sidebar display. There are multiple ways to set up page, post, and home page banners. The home page also has a full screen banner option. The theme comes pre-built with three color schemes: orange (default), green, and blue.

The theme is translation ready. It has also been optimized to work with several Jetpack features.
